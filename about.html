The Project

Built to close the gap between how customers search today, and how property details are actually shared

In Mumbai's 2BHK/3BHK market, property information usually moves through a chain — Sourcing Manager to Channel Partner to Telecaller to Customer — entirely over calls and WhatsApp. This site gives the customer a direct, self-service view of that same information: carpet area, price, location, amenities, and legal status, without waiting on agent availability.

01

What it replaces

Repeated phone calls just to compare two or three properties, with no independent reference point for the customer.

02

What it doesn't do

No online payments, no booking transactions, and no login-based CRM — legal/RERA verification still happens offline with the sales team, as is current practice.

03

How it's built

HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript on the front end, with property data managed as client-side JSON — lightweight, and fully functional without a separate database.
